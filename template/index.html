Our History
Mission & Vision

It is a historic hotel with a history of more than 100 years. Remodeling the old building, it has an old-fashioned appearance and modern interior facilities. Guest rooms are spacious, clean and have balcony with city views. There is a gallery where you can enjoy the works of art in the hotel. The roof top bar is great for sunbathing and relaxing. The reception operates currency exchange services, car rental services, and various tour reservation services.
